Joke #53

Pavin Fined?

This is from the article of David Cook (Daved_E._Cook@NOTES.PW.COM) in rec.sport.golf.

True Story. This morning, my girlfriend and I are driving to work listening to the radio. Sports news come on and I turn it up. Announcer says "...bla, bla, bla... Pavin wins it with a LATE CHARGE on the back..." Girlfriend asks, "How much is a late charge?" I didn't laugh then, and I hope she isn't a subscriber! dc
