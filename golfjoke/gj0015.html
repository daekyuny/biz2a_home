Joke #15

Golf Lesson 101 (1)

Me wife says to me one day "Ain't it about time you learned to play this golf thing that all the other husbands are playing?"

So I goes next door and says to me neighbor "Can you teach me to play golf?"

He: "Sure. Have you got any balls and club?"

Me: "?.....of course. Why?"

H: "Well bring'em to the club house tomorrow and we'll T-off."

M: "?T-off? What's this T-off?"

H: "Oh - it's just a golf term and we'll T-off right next to the clubhouse."

M: "Look, you T-off wherever you want to but I'll T-off in private if you don't mind."

H: "(chuckle) No no, a T's that little thing about the size of your little finger."

M: (them damn women been talking again)

H: "Look, the first thing you do is stick your T in the ground and put your ball on top of it."

M: "Oh, this is sit down game?"

H: "No, you're standing up when you put your ball on the T."

M: "Isn't that stretching things a bit far?"

H: "No. You got a bag to go along with your balls and clubs?"

M: "?.....of course. Why?"

H: "Zippered bag or velcro?"

M: "?...........neither."

H: "Oh, well how do you hold your club?"

M: "Two fingers."

H: "No, no. That's not right. Look let me get around behind you like this. Now spread your feet apart a bit. Bend over a bit. Now I'll put my arms around you and show you how to swing."

M: "Damn man, I spent six years in the Navy and I know what you got on your mind."

H: "Ok, look, you take your club and swing it over your shoulder..."

M: "No, no, that's me brother Jimmy you be thinking of."

H: "...and you hit your ball with it and it'll soar and soar."

M: "I can well believe that."

H: "Then when you're on the green..."

M: "What's the green thing?"

H: "Ah, that's where the hole is."

M: "You color blind?"

H: "No, why?" "...anyway, when you get there, you take your putter..."

M: "What's a putter?"

H: "Smallest club made"

M: (DAMN that woman, just can't keep her mouth shut).

H: "...and with it you put the ball in the hole."

M: "You mean the putter?"

H: "No, the ball, the hole isn't big enough for the ball and the putter."

M: "Well - that's when I knew he didn't know what he was talking about. Cuz I seen holes big enough for a horse-and-wagon."

H: "Then after the first hole, you go on to the next 17."

M: "I quit. Takes me 18 days to make one hole. Besides, how would I know when I'm in the 18th one?"

H: "Why, the hole's got a flag in it."

M: Sheeez!
