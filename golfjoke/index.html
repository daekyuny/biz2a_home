Forward

None of the jokes presented here are the original work of mine. These jokes have been floating around on the net for a few years, and I just compiled them and put some titles when needed. Please note that many of them might be offensive for various reasons, such as using explicit language, casting some religious characters and so on. It is therefore advised that you should not proceed to read these jokes if you think there is any possibility of being offended.

In building this page, most of the credits should be given to all the people who love the game of golf and particularly those who actively participate in ``rec.sports.golf'' news group. Your feedback and/or comments and/or any interesting articles are always welcome, and they are indeed the most important factor to keep this page as one of the enjoyable golfers' WEB pages. Enjoy them and send any feedback/new jokes to dkyoon@gmail.com.

Daekyun Yoon (Casual weekend golfer)
